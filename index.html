Growth Formula

We use the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) to model long-term growth:

\[ CAGR = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\frac{1}{\text{No. of Years}}} - 1 \]

This formula helps us smooth out market volatility for a clearer view of performance.
